About TenantScore System

Understanding how we evaluate and rate tenants and landlords

For Tenants

Your TenantScore is calculated based on several factors:

  • Payment History (40%)
  • Landlord Ratings (35%)
  • Length of Rental History (15%)
  • Credit Score (10%)

A higher score indicates a more reliable tenant with a strong rental history.

For Landlords

Landlord ratings are based on:

  • Property Maintenance
  • Communication
  • Responsiveness to Issues
  • Respect for Tenant Rights

These ratings help tenants make informed decisions about their future rentals.

Our Mission

We aim to create transparency and trust in the rental market by providing a fair and comprehensive rating system for both tenants and landlords. This helps create better rental relationships and promotes responsible behavior from all parties.
